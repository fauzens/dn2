ABOUT : Dune is a 2021 American romantic drama film directed by Castille Landon, with a screenplay by Sharon Soboil. Louise Lombard, Rob Estes, Arielle Kebbel, Chance Perdomo, Frances Turner, Kiana Madeira, Carter Jenkins and Mira Sorvino appear in supporting roles.
